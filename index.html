AvaDa: Avatar Dataset

Real large-scale dataset of human torsos captured on smartphone and external high-quality sensors

  • Download

Fill the gap in realistic avatar rendering and 3D reconstruction

Our dataset provides smartphone data (video and IMU) precisely synchronized with depth captured on Azure Kinect sensor as source of ground truth information for tasks of 3D reconstruction, rendering and single-shot depth estimation. Recordings were performed with blinking flash for possible usage in re-lightning tasks. Within the dataset, we deliver high-quality trajectories of the smartphone and segmentation masks for human avatars.

Real large-scale data

200 persons, 5 different poses per person, 4 semi-circle trials per pose

Data diversity

Variety of gender, ages and appearance (with complicated cases for rendering, i.e., glasses, beard)

Blinking flash during recording

For re-lightning modeling and usage in albedo and surface estimation
